About Me

I was born on 26th November 2004. I have been studying in Chittagong Grammar School for most of my life, and I feel grateful to my school for all the experiences that it has given me, leading me to become the person that I am today. I have been responsible for the founding of the Recreational Club of our school, working as a governing body member in the Programmng Club and for a year I had also been selected to become the Prefect of my class and serve the Student Body. For most of my school-life, I have been known through the school as the most enthusiastic one in the class as my personal philosophy has always made me repeat the words, "Knowledge knows no bounds".
